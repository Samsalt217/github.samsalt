LOT KIDZ






Our Goal

Thank you for visiting the LOT KIDZ space. we are on a mission to spread awarness of saftey and harm reduction in the festival scene. Weather you attend raves, folk fests, or any fun get together LOT KIDZ has you covered with apperal and more.

As a small group funded and supported by just a few friends we plan to bring coliquilly designed clothing and accessories to the festival scene. We are currently working on our first wave of protypes in clothing and accessories.

If you would like to support us, please consider donating or purchasing our merchandise when it becomes available. Every little bit helps us spread our message and create more designs. Feel free to reach out through the number provided to share your ideas and dreams.

Spread the word of LOT KIDZ by sharing via social media and admidst your friends.
